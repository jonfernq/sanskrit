
Flash card files to upload to flashcard sites are easy to create.
Different formats are possible with the word (face of card) 
and meaning (back of card) separated by a separator such as the comma 
or dash (',', '-'):

word - meaning
word - (grammar) meaning 

Though reveal.js is  being used to integrate interlinear translations
with vocabulary and grammar flashcards, 
here are some simple and quick flashcard sets which have been created:
